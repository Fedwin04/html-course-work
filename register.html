WELCOME TO FEDIE BIKERS CLUB

A community of passionate bikers

REGISTRATION

We are a community of passionate bikers united by our love for adventure, the open road and camaraderie. Founded in 2018, our club is dedicated to promoting safe and responsible biking practices while fostering a sense of belonging among our members. Our mission is to create unforgettable experiences through organized rides, charity events, and social gatherings, all while upholding the values of friendship, respect, and adventure. Whether you're an experienced rider or just starting your biking journey, we invite you to join us in exploring the thrill of the ride together.

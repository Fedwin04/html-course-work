Welcome to fedie bikers club
Gear up, start your engine, and join us on the ride of a lifetime!

Welcome to the Fedie Bikers Club! We are a community of passionate motorcycle enthusiasts who share a love for the open road and the thrill of riding. Whether you're a seasoned biker or just starting out, our club offers a welcoming and inclusive environment where you can connect with like-minded individuals, participate in exciting events, and explore the world on two wheels.

Our club was founded over a decade ago by a group of friends who wanted to create a space for bikers to come together, share their experiences, and support one another. Since then, we've grown into a vibrant community of riders from all walks of life, united by our common passion for the motorcycle lifestyle.
